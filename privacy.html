Privacy Policy

Article Extractor Pro

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Article Extractor Pro is a browser extension that extracts clean article text from web pages, removing ads, images, and navigation clutter. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the extension works.

The short version: Article Extractor Pro does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data processing happens entirely within your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics.

Data Collection

Article Extractor Pro does not collect any personal information. We do not track your browsing activity, and we do not send any data to external servers. The extension operates entirely offline within your browser.

What Data is Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. This data never leaves your device:

Extracted articles — When you extract an article, the title, body text, source URL, and basic metadata (author, date, word count) are saved in your browser's local storage. Articles are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

User preferences — Your settings such as dark mode, font size, quote highlighting, and copy format preferences are saved locally so they persist between sessions.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. No data leaves your browser. There are no analytics tools, no tracking pixels, no advertising SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind built into this extension.

Permissions Explained

The extension requires certain browser permissions to function. Here is a transparent explanation of each permission and why it is needed:

Permission Why It's Needed
activeTab To read the content of the web page you're currently viewing when you click "Extract Current Page." Only activates on your explicit action.
scripting To inject the content extraction script into web pages during batch processing. When you paste multiple URLs, the extension loads each page and runs a script to extract the article text.
storage To save extracted articles and your preferences locally in the browser. All stored data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.
tabs To create background tabs for batch extraction and to identify the currently active tab for one-click extraction.
alarms To schedule automatic cleanup of extracted articles older than 24 hours, keeping your browser storage clean.
sidePanel To provide an optional side panel viewing mode where you can read extracted articles alongside your browser window.
Host (all URLs) The content script needs to run on any website because users extract articles from any news source. It only activates when you explicitly trigger extraction.

Third-Party Services

Article Extractor Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The only external resource loaded is Google Fonts (for the extension's UI typography), which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Article Extractor Pro does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension does not collect information from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
